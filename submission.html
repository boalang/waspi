(WASPI)

Monday, October 30, 2017 - co-located with ASE 2017

Submission Information

WASPI 2017 invites contributions in the form of 4-page papers from both researchers and practitioners. Submissions can be research papers, practice papers, position papers, or experience reports. All submissions should describe unpublished work and must have been neither previously accepted for publication nor concurrently submitted for review in another journal, book, conference, or workshop. Submissions are peer-reviewed and accepted papers will appear in the workshop proceedings.

Papers should be submitted electronically at: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=waspi2017

Submissions must follow the ASE author instructions and be no more than 4 pages (including references).

Contact: rdyer@bgsu.edu
